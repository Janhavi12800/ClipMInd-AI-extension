# Product Hunt Launch Plan

## Launch Date
Target: Tuesday or Wednesday, 12:01 AM Pacific Time

## Pre-Launch (T-7 days)

### Assets
- [ ] Product Hunt thumbnail (240×240)
- [ ] Gallery images (5 screenshots, 1270×760)
- [ ] 60-second demo video (GIF or MP4)
- [ ] Maker comment draft (see below)
- [ ] First comment with special launch offer

### Hunter
- Recruit a top hunter (500+ followers) or self-hunt
- Brief hunter 48 hours before launch with product details

### Community
- [ ] Notify email waitlist (send 24h before)
- [ ] Prepare Twitter/X thread (10 tweets)
- [ ] Prepare LinkedIn post
- [ ] Alert team/supporters to upvote at launch (not brigading)
