# Privacy Policy — TradePrompt AI

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Overview

TradePrompt AI ("we", "our", "the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that generates AI-powered trading analysis prompts. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Stored Locally on Your Device
- AI API keys (OpenAI/Claude) — stored in Chrome's encrypted sync storage
- Extension settings (market preference, risk %, capital)
- License key and subscription status
- Daily usage counters

**We never transmit your API keys to our servers.**

### Transmitted to Our Backend
- Email address (only when you subscribe)
- License key (for verification)
- Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay — we do not store card/UPI details

### Transmitted to AI Providers
- Generated prompts and chart screenshots are sent directly from your browser to OpenAI or Anthropic using your API key
- We do not proxy or store AI requests

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Trading history or portfolio data
- Personal financial information
- Browsing history outside TradingView
- Chart data (processed locally and via your AI provider only)

## Third-Party Services
- **OpenAI / Anthropic** — AI analysis (your API key)
- **Razorpay** — Payment processing
- **TradingView** — Chart platform (content script reads visible chart info only)
- **CoinGecko / Yahoo Finance** — Optional market price data

## Data Retention
- License data: retained while subscription is active + 90 days after cancellation
- Local extension data: until you uninstall the extension

## Your Rights
- Delete local data: uninstall the extension
- Cancel subscription: via Razorpay dashboard or email us
- Request data deletion: contact support

## Children's Privacy
This extension is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

## Changes
We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
