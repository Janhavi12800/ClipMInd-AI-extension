# Privacy Policy

**TechShield AI**  
**Effective Date:** June 15, 2026  
**Last Updated:** June 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

TechShield AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the TechShield AI Chrome extension and web platform (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information**: Name, email address, password (hashed), profile details
- **Payment information**: Processed by Stripe and Razorpay — we do not store card numbers
- **User content**: AI prompts, notes, saved content, scan preferences

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Extension usage**: Feature usage, scan counts, error logs (anonymized)
- **Website analysis data**: Page title, meta tags, headings, technology signatures (only when you initiate a scan)
- **Device information**: Browser type, extension version, operating system
- **Log data**: IP address (hashed), request timestamps, API endpoints accessed

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Complete browsing history
- Passwords, credit card numbers entered on websites
- Personal files or downloads
- Keystrokes or form input (except URLs you explicitly save as notes)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Service
- Process subscriptions and payments
- Analyze website security, SEO, and technology (on your request)
- Generate and enhance AI prompts
- Send service-related communications
- Improve the Service through anonymized analytics
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security
- Extension data is stored locally via Chrome storage APIs
- All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- IP addresses are hashed before storage
- We implement access controls, audit logging, and regular security reviews

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- **Payment processors**: Stripe, Razorpay (for billing)
- **Infrastructure providers**: Supabase, cloud hosting (for service delivery)
- **Analytics**: Anonymized, aggregated usage data
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data (prompts, notes, scans)
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact: **privacy@techshield.ai**

## 7. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Scan results: Retained for 90 days (configurable in settings)
- Audit logs: Retained for 1 year
- Payment records: Retained as required by tax law (7 years)
- Deleted account data: Purged within 30 days

## 8. Chrome Extension Specific

The TechShield AI Chrome extension:
- Processes webpage data locally for security and SEO analysis
- Only accesses page content when you initiate a scan or open the side panel
- Stores prompts and notes locally; syncs to cloud only when signed in
- Does not track browsing across sites without your action
- Displays clear warnings for detected phishing/malware (does not block silently)

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.
