# TechShield AI — Growth Roadmap

Strategic growth plan from launch through enterprise scale.

## Vision

Become the default AI-powered security and productivity extension for professionals, teams, and enterprises — reaching $1M ARR within 18 months of launch.

## Pricing Strategy

| Plan | Price | Target Segment | Key Value |
|------|-------|----------------|-----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Individual users, trial | 10 prompts/day, basic security |
| **Pro** | $19/mo ($150/yr) | Power users, freelancers | Unlimited prompts, full security suite |
| **Business** | $49/mo ($470/yr) | Teams up to 25 seats | Collaboration, audit logs, priority support |

### Pricing experiments (Q2–Q3)

- Annual discount optimization (target 20–35% savings)
- Team seat-based pricing for Business (per-seat add-on)
- India-specific pricing via Razorpay (₹999/mo Pro, ₹2,499/mo Business)

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Chrome Web Store rejection | High | Pre-review compliance, minimal permissions |
| Low free-to-paid conversion | High | In-app upgrade prompts, usage limits |
| Competitor launches similar product | Medium | Speed to market, unique security+AI combo |
| Payment fraud | Medium | Stripe Radar, webhook verification |
| API cost overrun (AI tokens) | Medium | Usage metering, plan limits |

## Success Metrics Dashboard

Track weekly in Grafana + business analytics:

1. **Acquisition**: Installs, signups, traffic sources
2. **Activation**: First scan, first prompt, onboarding completion
3. **Revenue**: MRR, ARPU, conversion rate, churn
4. **Retention**: DAU/MAU, feature usage, NPS
5. **Referral**: Affiliate signups, referral program usage
